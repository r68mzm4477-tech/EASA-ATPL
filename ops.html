Operational Procedures

EASA Operational Knowledge

  • Hypoxia risk increases significantly with altitude.
  • Smoke and fumes are treated as time-critical emergencies.
  • Anti-ice prevents ice formation; de-ice removes existing ice.
  • Wake turbulence separation is essential behind heavy aircraft.
  • CRM improves operational safety and decision-making.
  • Rejected takeoffs above high speed require careful judgement.
